<p>例如，给出&nbsp;<em>n </em>=<em> </em>3，生成结果为：</p>

  &quot;((()))&quot;,
  &quot;(()())&quot;,
  &quot;(())()&quot;,
  &quot;()(())&quot;,
  &quot;()()()&quot;
